Start Date -- End Date: Oct. 15 2019 -- Dec. 1 2019

A project for the IEEE chapter at UCSD's quarterly project competition of Fall 2019. The goal of the competition was to create a product that would help students with school with a team of 4 or 5. The project's aim was to allow users to submit their schedules and create and join groups to receive possible meeting times.

Roles in the collaborative project:
  • Responsible for coordinating a team of 4 with varying levels of computer programming experience.
  • Used Heroku for our backend and Java for Android for the frontend of our app.
  • Developed a REST API using Falcon webserver connected to PostgreSQL databse.
